Mary Lennox,  was little girl, is the protagonist of  story. Her father was always busy and ill and her mother cared only to go to parties.
Mary was selfish and irritable by nature. The young English governess who came  teach her and had left her place in 3 months only.
Mary’s parents died of cholera and she was sent to Mr. Archibald Craven, her uncle and his wife, who was her father’s sister.
Mr Craven was weird by nature. He cared about nobody and most of the time he shut himself up  a room where except Pitcher no one was allowed 2 enter.
